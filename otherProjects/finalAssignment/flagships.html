Flagships



2016 Lexus RC

From $48,350* This 3.5 L V6 creates 255HP while seating four. Available in 4 and 8 colour options for the interior and exterior respectively.

A Top Speed of 233 km/h and a 0-100km/h time of 5.7 seconds. This car has sport inspiration but remains a luxury car at it's core.

The Upgraded F-Sport package provides the stiff platform needed to allow Lexus to use more aggressive suspension tuning while still maintaining highway ride quality.



2016 Lexus GS

From $56,550 this 3.5 L V6 creates 311HP while seating 5. Available in 10 and 9 colour options for the exterior and interior respectively.

Safety is Key and the GS protects you with 10 airbages, in addition to hefty brakes: 14 inch diameter two-piece front rotors & four-piston brake calipers.



2016 Lexus RX

Comfort and Interior Quality are still key strengths for the redesigned 2016 Lexus RX 350, and a more car-like driving experience helps broaden its appeal.

Supremely Quiet V6 Engine and an appealing ride and handling balance. Seats 5.
